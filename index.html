SLIIT-Innovate

Empowering Innovation and Entrepeneurship in Sri Lanka in the 21st Century

SLIIT-Innovate is a combination of the University Business Linkage (UBL) cell, HEI cell, and SLIIT Concept Nusery, of the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). We facilitate and provide assistance with the following, for both our students and outside collaborators.

  • Startup formation under our Incubator/Concept Nursery
  • University Business Linkage and Industry Engagement
  • IP, Patenting, Revenue Sharing Models, MoUs, etc.

If you are an undergraduate, postgraduate or research student, or member of staff at SLIIT or any state or private insitution in Sri Lanka, please contact us for assistance.


Mission

Our mission is tempted to create innovators by Enrich Teaching, Learning and Research Experince. By recognizing creativity and Innovation, We support to Industry focused Research and Commercialize Research Outcomes.
