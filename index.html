SLIIT-Innovate

Empowering Innovation and Entrepeneurship in Sri Lanka in the 21st Century

SLIIT Innovate is an Institute level overarching platform that synergizes the activities of SLIIT business linkage activities, SLIIT-Society Cell, SLIIT Incubator/Concept Nursery of the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). We facilitate and provide assistance with the following, for both our students and outside collaborators.

  • Startup formation under our Incubator/Concept Nursery
  • University Business Linkage and Industry Engagement
  • IP, Patenting, Revenue Sharing Models, MoUs, etc.

If you are an undergraduate, postgraduate or research student, or member of staff at SLIIT or any state or private insitution in Sri Lanka, please contact us for assistance.

Welcome to The Sri Lanka Technology Incubator

The Sri Lanka Technology Incubator (SLTI) also known as Concept nursery is located at SLIIT Malabe Campus which was established in 2002 to promote, assist innovators and establish innovations and startup companies by providing access to business and technical expertise, financial resources, international industry contacts, communication facilities, business advice, business plan development, and marketing. The connected Companies/establishments use the Institute’s state-of-the-art facilities including modern networking infrastructure and will have access to a pool of expertise and well trained capable students to carry out research and development. Selected students will have the opportunity to work in this facility by engaging themselves in research and development projects undertaken by these resident companies. This exposure will provide the students with valuable experience in a working environment.
